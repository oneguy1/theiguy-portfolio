Man vs. Machine: The AI Hype I

Part 1: Welcome to the New World

In late November 2022, something remarkable happened—a game-changer in how we approach daily tasks and problem-solving.

The release of ChatGPT showcased the power of AI and sparked widespread speculation about its impact on the workforce. From day one, it made waves—simplifying tasks like rewriting texts and tackling complex, thought-provoking questions that tested its boundaries.

As these tools evolve, the conversation about their role in the job market grows louder. Chatbots, once limited to simple queries, now often serve as the first point of contact between customers and companies. With the advent of advanced AI, these tools are set to go even further.

But this raises a critical question: Whose job is on the line?
